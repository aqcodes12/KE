FOUR GREAT REASONS

TO CHOOSE US.

GERMAN TECHNOLOGY

VEKA profiles are a product of German engineering. They come with co-extruded Thermo-plastic Vulcanised(TPV) gaskets. This unique gasket welds seamlessly with the corner joint and leads to better thermal & sound insulation, adding to the aesthetic appeal of your windows. Glazing beads co-extruded with soft PVC gasket in colours to match the TPV gasket provides durability and better grip to the glass.

FLEXIBLE DESIGN OPTIONS

VEKA doors and windows are custom designed for India. Which means that they have outward opening and have provisions for installing grills and insect mesh. Virtually any design, shape, size and colours are possible with NCL VEKA.

END-TO-END SOLUTION

VEKA has the largest dedicated fabricator network in the country who are trained in fabricating different types of doors & windows. They provide end-to-end service right from suggesting the right profiles to fabricating them and then finally installing them. So that you do not have to worry at any stage.

UNMATCHED SERVICE QUALITY

Technical Support Teams of the company are deployed at different locations to facilitate proximity with fabricators. The Technical Support Team periodically visits the fabricating units and audit the process followed to ensure compliance with the specifications and process guidelines laid down by the company.
