Frequently Asked Questions?

The pricing for uPVC front doors depends on your individual requirements. High-quality uPVC doors are widely known for their exceptional performance and durability which makes them a high-value investment. Also, prices will vary if you opt for different customization options.

uPVC doors are an excellent choice if you are looking for a door that is secured, durable, stylish and easy to maintain. Unlike traditional materials like wood and steel, uPVC does not rot, rust, jam or warp. It looks as good as new for years without any painting or polishing. High-quality uPVC doors come with multi-point locking, a multichambered design, and high wall thickness to make the doors strong and secure. Additonally, uPVC doors come in a range of designs, colours and laminate options, giving you ample of choice in terms of styling your space.

While PVC stands for Polyvinyl chloride, uPVC is Unplasticized Polyvinyl Chloride- a functionally superior, non-toxic material that is extensively used in the construction industry. PVC, due to its chemical composition, is less durable and more toxic for the environment whereas uPVC is highly durable and eco-friendly.

Different types of uPVC doors come at different pricing. However, high-quality uPVC doors and windows are functionally superior and require little to no maintenance cost and effort, thus making them affordable to buy, install and maintain. Prices may also vary depending on the customization options you choose.

Modern uPVC doors and windows, are constructed using hardwearing materials making them strong and durable. They are also made with an insulating multi-chamber interior which is both strong and high-functioning. Additionally, multi-point locks also add to the security of a uPVC door. Sash jammers also help in extra security for any uPVC door, they are not only cheap but are also a quick way to upgrade the security of your place.

Most uPVC product companies guarantee their products for 10 years. However, uPVC doors by brands that use high-quality raw materials and world-class technology can be durable for upto 35 years with minimal maintenance.
