SPECIALITIES

ARCH WINDOWS

VEKA I-60 Casement profiles have the flexibility to be bent into arch shapes which gives a traditional look to the building. Our in-house arch bending facility is capable of fabricating arch windows with options of single and double glazing.

TILT & TURN WINDOWS

VEKA Tilt & Turn Windows provide an avant-garde look to your house. These are inward opening windows available in I-60 Casement Series and ensure good ventilation to your home. The windows provide a higher degree of flexibility which is suited for any building type.

  • 8 mm sash overlap for superior performance
  • Imported hardware of high corrosion resistance properties
  • Multi-point locking with hardware screw fixed to steel
  • Glazing options - 6 mm single glazing / 20 & 24 mm double glazing

SLIDE & FOLD DOORS

VEKA Slide & Fold Doors offer a premium look with the maximum opening space which can be integrated into your balconies or terrace opening. The window system is available in I-60 Casement Series with German hardware

  • 8 mm sash overlap for superior performance
  • Multi-point locking with hardware screw fixed to steel
  • Heavy duty roller for smooth operation
  • Glazing options - 6 mm single glazing / 20 & 24 mm double glazing

TILT & SLIDE DOORS

VEKA Tilt & Slide Doors offer a grand view while giving the option to tilt and slide the doors behind the fixed panel for maximum ventilation. The door system is in I-60 Casement Series with European hardware which makes it reliable, soundproof and secure.

  • 8 mm sash overlap for superior performance
  • Multi-point locking with hardware screw fixed to steel
  • eavy duty roller for smooth operation
  • Glazing options - 6 mm single glazing / 20 & 24 mm double glazing

TWIN SASH GRILL & MESH WINDOWS

VEKA Twin Sash Grill & Mesh Windows are aesthetically superior. The window system is available in I-60 Casement series with multi-point locking hardware that provides safety and security besides keeping insects out. It also comes with an option of grill only, without mosquito screen.

  • 8 mm Sash overlap for Superior performance
  • Outward opening glass panel and inward opening mesh panel
  • Glazing options - 6 mm single glazing / 20 & 24 mm double glazing

SPECIAL APPLICATIONS

Parallel Windows

The ideal choice for maximum ventilation. VEKA Parallel Windows are safe and secure for even children’s rooms, as they open only 135 mm (5 inches). And to top that, they’re easy to operate.

Vertical Slider

Their American style of operation makes these windows ideal for residential developments. They do not take up inside or outside space, as the shutters are sliding and can be held at any position, from fully open to closed and everything in between.

Bay Windows

Bay Windows are combinations of two or more windows connected at an angle (90 to 180 degrees) or in a curved shape. It provides a panoramic outside view and adds aesthetic value to a home.

Motorised/Lever Operated Windows

Ideal for windows installed at a height that is not accessible to operate. The lever handle can be combined to operate up to three shutters at the same time. A motorised option is also available

ACCESSORIES

ESPAGNOLETTES

Made out of carbon steel with high corrosion resistant coating, VEKA espagnolettes are conformed by EN 1670, Grade 5 (480 hours for red rust) and are available in several sizes:

  • Casement window in 600, 800, 1000 & 1200, 1600 mm & 1800 mm
  • Sliding door in 1200, 1600, 1800 & 2000 mm

HANDLES

Zinc / Aluminium alloy handles in White (RAL9016) / Dark Brown (RAL 8022) finish:

  • Cockspur handle (left & right) for single point locking
  • Casement window handle (L-shaped) for multi-point locking
  • Cranked casement window handle (low-neck) for windows with grill and mesh
  • Casement door handle for integrated lock (espag)
  • Special touch locks with strikers
  • Patio handle (Pop-up Handle), L-shaped, low-neck and D type handles

HINGES

  • SS430 grade friction hinges in 8”, 10”, 12”, 16”, 20” & 24” (SS304 also available on request)
  • 3D hinges, butt hinges & concealed hinges

ROLLERS

  • Single roller (.at) 16 mm width x 14 mm height
  • Single roller (grooved) 12 mm width x 17 / 21 mm height, nylon body with ball bearing.
  • Adjustable double roller (grooved) 12 mm width x 17.5/23 mm height. SS304 with ball bearing
  • Roller for Y-screen sashes

WOOL PILES

Silicone grade waterproof and UV resistant, available in 6 x 6 mm, 5 x 8 mm and 6 x 10 mm

SCREWS & FASTENERS

Carbon steel screws (self-drilling & self-tapping) with high corrosion resistant coating in various sizes for window/door fabrication, SS410 screws for friction hinges and frame anchor expansion bolt 8 x 100 mm for installation
