At Venster, you can choose from the various glass options available and select the one right for your space.

Glass options

Tinted glass along with adding an aesthetic appeal to your space also filters the sunlight and keeps your home cool at all times.

Laminated glass is made by bonding two or more glass with one or multiple layers of polyvinylbutyral (PVB). Laminated glass is the best available option if you are looking for enhanced safety.

One of the most popular and multipurpose glass options, clear glass, as the name suggests, provides a clear view inside as well as outside. Also, you can customize the glass with the thickness of your choice.

Frosted glass creates a blurred and translucent effect, meaning you cannot see the image clearly but still enjoy the sunlight. Frosted glass is best suited for space needing visual privacy. It is best used in a double glazing format

Glazing types

Single glazing

Single glazing includes just one single layer of glass.

Double glazing

In double glazing, two layers of glass are sealed together with an air space left in between the two for better thermal performance.

Triple glazing

Triple glazing refers to three layers of glass sealed together with an air space left in between the three layers for better thermal performance.

Depending on your need and preference, you can choose the glass with the glazing of your choice.

Mesh & grill options

To give you an extra and functional layer of security, Venster windows and doors come with an option to add a mesh or a grill. Along with enhancing your security, the mesh also protects your space from mosquitoes and improves the ventilation

Mesh options

S S Mesh

SS mesh is very strong and durable and it offers clear visibility to the outside world. SS mesh is apt if you are looking for improved ventilation. Also, on the plus side, the SS mesh is free of corrosion and rust.

Fiber Mesh

Vinyl-coated fiber screen is widely used as a mesh material in windows and doors. It does not corrode, dent, rust or unravel. It is affordable and easy to install.

You can get the mesh of your choice and install it in the style you prefer – openable or sliding.

Grill options

Contrary to the installation of the grill in traditional home windows, Venster comes with an option to fix the grill on the uPVC frame itself. You can customize your windows and doors with your design of the grill.

Hardware options

All our uPVC windows and doors give you the option of complementing the windows and doors with the hardware of your choice. Right from clean and modern to stylish and smooth to operate, you can customize the handles and locks on the basis of your need and reference.
